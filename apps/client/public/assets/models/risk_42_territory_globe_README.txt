RISK-STYLE 42-TERRITORY TRANSPARENT GLOBE

Files
- risk_42_territory_globe.glb: 42 separately named territory mesh objects.
- risk_42_territory_globe_manifest.json: continent, army/label anchor and classic adjacency data.

Design
- No ocean sphere or sea geometry.
- Original spherical territory boundaries generated from geographic land data and 42 gameplay regions.
- Antarctica is omitted.
- Radius is 1.0 unit; anchors sit at radius 1.025.
- Territory meshes are surface shells and can be extruded inward in your engine or DCC package.

Important
This is an original game-development mesh inspired by the familiar 42-region world-conquest structure.
It is not a scan or reproduction of Hasbro's board artwork.
